About Us

Welcome to our financial habits building platform! We believe that everyone, regardless of their financial background, deserves the tools and knowledge to manage their money effectively.

Our mission is to simplify personal finance, making it accessible and actionable for first-jobbers and final-year students. We know how daunting it can be to start saving, budgeting, and planning for the future, which is why we've designed a simple, visual, and intuitive platform to guide you every step of the way.

With our app, you can:

  • Plan your money with clear spending limits and dedicated savings goals for fun and emergencies.
  • Track every expense, loan, and debt to see exactly where your money is going.
  • Visualize your financial progress with easy-to-understand charts and summaries.

We're passionate about empowering you to take control of your financial life. Join us today and start building healthy financial habits that will last a lifetime!
